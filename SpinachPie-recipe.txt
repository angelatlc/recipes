Spinach Frittata Pie

16oz chopped spinach
8 eggs (beaten)
1/2 cup chopped onions
1/2 tbsp salt


Preheat oven to 375 degrees and grease a well seasoned cast iron frying pan.
Saute onions until translucent.  Add spinach and saute until wilted.  Add salt and beaten eggs.  Mix well and pour into cast iron pan.
Bake for 20-30 minutes or until a toothpick stuck in the center of the pie comes out clean.
